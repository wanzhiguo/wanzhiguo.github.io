Zhiguo Wan received the B.S. degree in computer science from Tsinghua University in 2002 and the Ph.D. degree in information security from the National University of Singapore in 2007 (Supervisor: Prof. Robert H. Deng). He is a Principal Investigator with the Zhejiang Laboratory, Hangzhou, Zhejiang, China. He was Associate Professor with Shandong University (2015-2021), Assistant Professor with Tsinghua University (2008-2015), and Postdoc with Katholieke University of Leuven, Belgium (2006-2008).

He has published more than 100 peer-reviewed conference and journal articles, including ACM CCS, INFOCOM, IEEE TRANSACTIONS ON DEPENDABLE AND SECURE COMPUTING, IEEE TRANSACTIONS ON INFORMATION FORENSICS AND SECURITY, IEEE TRANSACTIONS ON MOBILE COMPUTING, and IEEE TRANSACTIONS ON PARALLEL AND DISTRIBUTED SYSTEMS. His main research interests include security and privacy for distributed systems, such as cloud computing, the Internet of Things, and blockchain. He has been a PC Member of dozens of international conferences, including IEEE INFOCOM. He serves on the editorial board for the IEEE OPEN JOURNAL OF THE COMMUNICATIONS SOCIETY.

现任之江实验室研究员/研究专家(正高)，毕业于清华大学和新加坡国立大学，中国计算机学会高级会员，中国计算机学会区块链专委首任委员，主要研究方向为区块链、云计算、大数据、物联网安全和隐私保护。作为项目负责人主持国家自然科学基金项目4项，以及山东省自然科学基金重大基础研究项目、面上项目、实验室内部揭榜挂帅项目和国家任务、教育部留学回国人员基金项目等多项项目；作为项目骨干参与国家自然科学基金重点项目，科技部重点研发计划项目。 已发表学术论文100多篇，包括ACM CCS, INFOCOM，IEEE TDSC, IEEE TIFS等CCF A类顶级国际会议和国际期刊，申请专利20余项，授权国家发明专利10余项、美国发明专利1项。Google Scholar引用次数达4000余次，单篇引用次数达700+次。出版《智能计算》专著一部，获得2022年浙江省科技进步二等奖。
